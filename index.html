MISSION ZONES

Choose Your Depth

0 - 200M

ZONE 01

Sunlight Zone

This part of the ocean is full of life because sunlight allows plants to grow, but it is also the most at risk. When you look at the coral reefs and schools of tropical fish, you can see the impact of human activity, from plastic waste in the water to coral bleaching caused by rising sea temperatures. XplorOcean starts in the Sunlight Zone, where survival and sunlight meet.

200 - 1000M

ZONE 02

Twilight Zone

Travel deeper into a dim world, where the largest animal migration on Earth takes place. In this darker zone, glowing jellyfish and lanternfish are important for the carbon cycle of the ocean. But even here, microplastics have started to enter the food web. Learn how what we do on the surface can upset the balance of this mysterious zone.

1000 - 4000M

ZONE 03

Midnight Zone

Discover a place of complete darkness and intense pressure, where life survives in tough conditions. Although it seems far away, the Midnight Zone collects many of the pollutants. Meet unique predators and learn how deep-sea mining and pollution threaten a habitat we are only starting to explore.
